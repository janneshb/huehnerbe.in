## Thesis Supervision

I supervise all types of theses on Bachelor's and Master's level, together with <a href="https://www.professoren.tum.de/en/romano-marcello" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Prof. Romano</a> or Prof. Schmucker at the Technical University of Munich.

For more information, have a look at the <a href="https://www.asg.ed.tum.de/en/coa/thesis-opportunities/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">thesis opportunities</a> at the Chair of Astrodynamics or get in touch <a href="mailto:jannes.huehnerbein@tum.de">via email</a> if you would like to propose your own topic.
