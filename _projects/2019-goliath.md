As a team of five undergraduate students, we won first place in the "Innovation Project" at ETH Zurich, competing against more than 500 fellow students.

The Innovation Project ("Innovationsprojekt") is a hands-on design course in the mechanical engineering Bachelor's program at ETH Zurich. In teams, students design and build a fully functional mechanical system that solves a given task.

The two-semester project culminates in a grand finale in the great hall of the ETH main building, where all teams compete against each other in a tournament.

Our team won first place out of more than 500 participating students. The video below gives an impression of the project and the finale:
